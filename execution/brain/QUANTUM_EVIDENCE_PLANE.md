# Quantum evidence plane

Status: PARTIALLY OPERATIONAL

`GET /api/a11oy/v1/brain/quantum-evidence` unifies the estate's quantum-related
capabilities without collapsing their evidence levels. Every source is typed as
classical computation, simulation, formal witness, instrument-fed model, hardware
measurement, evidence policy, or research inspiration.

Current evidence supports executable classical gates, simulations, deterministic
models, formal witnesses, and validation contracts. It does not support a verified
QPU execution, a trained quantum model, physical validation of the attached paper,
or quantum advantage.

## Original proposal/acceptance contract

The paper's broad two-sided transaction idea inspires an engineering protocol:

1. An offer binds task digest, classical baseline, budget, backend plan, and provenance.
2. A confirmation binds the same task digest to measured output, uncertainty, raw
   artifact digest, authorized acceptor, and receipt.
3. Commit occurs only if both sides bind, the run ledger reconciles, and policy passes.

This is an original evidence-governance abstraction. It is not a simulation or adoption
of advanced/retarded electromagnetic potentials and makes no faster-than-light claim.
