# Rejected patterns

- Scraping private or access-controlled content, credentials, prompts, or proprietary code.
- Copying a competitor's interface, branding, identity, or unverifiable marketing claims.
- Treating similarity search as truth or a graph visualization as operational evidence.
- Silent cross-tenant memory, automatic training use, or propagation without admission.
- Overwriting contradictory evidence or hiding negative benchmark results.
- Claiming AGI, ASI, sentience, autonomous evolution, trained weights, or live inference
  without reproducible artifacts and independent verification.
- Weakening branch protection or fabricating independent review to accelerate deployment.
