# Original differentiation

The proposed SZL brain is a governed evidence plane spanning code, deployments, Hugging Face
artifacts, and operational outcomes. Its unit is not a chat message or vector: it is a scoped
record with provenance, epistemic status, classification, lifecycle, integrity, and explicit
training and propagation permissions.

The differentiator is the closed evidence loop: observe, admit, retrieve, propose, sandbox,
authorize, deploy, measure, and remember. Each boundary has a receipt and each public claim
has a status. Holographic visualization becomes a view over that contract, never proof by itself.

External leader systems enter this loop only as revision-pinned research records. A Chinese
or Western provider becomes useful after its model interface, tool behavior, context limits,
license, hardware requirements, and benchmark results pass the same admission contract. That
turns "fashion thinking" into an auditable synthesis process: principles may transfer, but
weights, code, branding, prompts, and claims do not transfer without explicit provenance and
permission.
