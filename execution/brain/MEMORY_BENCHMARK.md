# Memory benchmark

Status: PLANNED

The locked benchmark will contain public, synthetic, and licensed records spanning
episodic, semantic, procedural, evidence, decision, and outcome memory. It measures
admission precision, provenance completeness, duplicate detection, contradiction recall,
scope isolation, deletion propagation, and temporal freshness.

Release gates: zero seeded-secret admissions, zero cross-tenant leaks, 100% provenance
on returned evidence, at least 90% contradiction recall, and verified deletion from all
online indexes. Dataset revision, runner revision, environment, and raw results must be
published together. No score is claimed until those artifacts exist.
