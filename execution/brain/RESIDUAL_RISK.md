# Residual risk

The schema and validator do not provide storage, authentication, encryption, signed
receipts, secret scanning, graph consistency, index deletion, or production availability.
Public connector inventories can change after audit. Model and dataset names do not prove
weights, training, benchmark quality, or inference. Visual graph and 3D brain surfaces can
mislead users unless the capability endpoint remains visible and current.

The largest immediate operational risks are an invalid Hugging Face write credential,
no eligible independent GitHub reviewer, deployment/source drift, and the absence of a
durable receipt store. Production promotion should remain gated on those items.
