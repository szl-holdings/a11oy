## Paragraph to insert in §10.0 (just after the four-sentence thesis frame)

This chapter does not stand alone. The knot-calculus reading of Λ
presupposes the §1.1.1 inversion that *receipts are the canonical record
of behavior*: the receipt-chain DAG IS the audit artifact, not a
side-channel log of an event that happened elsewhere. The braid-group
reading of multi-actor concurrency (Birman 1974) and the chord-diagram
reading of pendant-cord hierarchy (Bar-Natan 1995) name the structural
primitives of an object that, by §1.1.1, is the only authoritative trace.
Equivalently: the audit-Reidemeister moves R1/R2/R3 are well-posed as
statements about Λ because §1.1.2 fixes Λ at compile time of the
runtime module — they would not be meaningful statements about a
caller-parameterised gate. The two inversions are the *substrate* on
which the knot-calculus reframe is performed.
