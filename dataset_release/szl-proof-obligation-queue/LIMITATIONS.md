# Limitations

1. **Unpublished.** DOI and DOI URL are `PENDING`. No archive deposition or
   readback has occurred.
2. **Not peer reviewed.** Packaging and tests are local engineering evidence,
   not scholarly review or independent replication.
3. **No new mathematical proof.** The queue preserves source status. Only two
   crosswalk records have local item-bound `KERNEL_ACCEPTED` status; 144 require
   action or preserve a refutation.
4. **Namespace collisions remain.** `F1` through `F23` are reused for different
   statements. This package blocks same-ID proof transfer.
5. **Holdout only.** Every tranche row has `training_eligible=false`. The
   package is not a model-training corpus and was not used to train or promote a
   model.
6. **No raw Brain corpus.** The 9,464 observed raw nodes remain excluded. The
   package contains only a receipt-scoped summary of the five-document local
   retrieval pilot.
7. **No latency claim.** The source pilot did not commit an end-to-end latency
   measurement; no two-second or equivalent response-time claim is supported.
8. **Limited external validity.** The 15-query pilot is deterministic and
   local. Its metrics do not establish performance on other corpora, systems,
   users, or domains.
9. **Unsigned receipt.** SHA-256 content receipts detect byte changes but do not
   authenticate an identity. No DSSE, in-toto, Sigstore, or transparency-log
   entry is claimed for this package.
10. **Rights review required.** Apache-2.0 covers package-authored metadata,
    schemas, and transformations. It does not cure unknown or incompatible
    rights in upstream material. Public release remains blocked pending
    item-level review and human approval.
