It signs its work

Every governed decision is sealed as a DSSE receipt — ECDSA‑P256 signed and SHA3‑256 hash-chained into an append-only ledger. The signature is checked in your browser with WebCrypto against a key you fetch yourself. Receipts are signed on write, never on a read.

It refuses when unsure

A self-doubt gate sits in front of the answer. The inverse-PINN physics and materials engine measures its own uncertainty against a bound — and returns a truthful BLOCKED rather than a confident wrong answer when it crosses it. A truthful refusal beats a fabricated pass.
