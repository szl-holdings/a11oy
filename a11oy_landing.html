We grade what we say. If a claim isn't MEASURED live this session, we tell you exactly what it is instead of dressing it up as proof.

MEASURED

Read live from a running endpoint this session — signed-receipt count, advisory Λ posture, chain depth. Shown with a live chip; a dead probe degrades to an honest offline chip.

REPORTED

UNKNOWN

We don't have it right now. Rendered honestly as N/A · UNAVAILABLE · BLOCKED — never a confident guess and never a fabricated number.

CONJECTURE

Advisory and unproven. Rendered in gray, never green, never a theorem. Λ lives here.

The thesis

Governed AI you can prove — not AI you're asked to trust.

Most AI asks for trust. a11oy earns it the way auditable systems do: every governed decision is sealed into a signed, hash-chained receipt; a trust gate can only tighten an answer, never wave it through; and the kernel rests on machine-checked theorems you can read. The honest part is the product — when a claim isn't proven, we label it, and you can open the check yourself.

01 · SIGNS

It signs its work

DSSE receipts, ECDSA-P256 signed and SHA3-256 hash-chained into an append-only ledger. Signed on write, never on a read. Verify them in your own browser.

02 · REFUSES

It refuses when unsure

A self-doubt gate measures uncertainty against a bound and returns a truthful BLOCKED rather than a confident wrong answer. A refusal beats a fabrication.

03 · PROVES

It is formally backed

8 locked, axiom-free Lean 4 theorems back the kernel. The Λ trust gate is Conjecture 1 — an advisory bound, never dressed up as a theorem.
