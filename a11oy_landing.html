What gets checked

1 · POST /api/a11oy/v1/govern/infer — a real governed turn.
2 · GET /cosign.pub — the in-image public key (PEM).
3 · Rebuild the DSSE PAE, recompute SHA‑256, run crypto.subtle.verify.

DER→raw r‖s conversion happens in-browser; the private key never leaves the box.

Why it matters

A signature anyone can check, against a key they fetched themselves, is the difference between “trust us” and proof. The same logic backs every receipt in the ledger.
