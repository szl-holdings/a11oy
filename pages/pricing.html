Do I have to trust your numbers?

No. Every decision ships a signed receipt you can re-verify yourself, in your own browser, at /verify.

Can it run fully offline?

Yes — the Enterprise tier deploys air-gapped on your own hardware with no runtime dependency on outside services. Open-weight models only.

What is "proven"?

Five core formulas (the canonical locked set) are proven in Lean 4 and re-checked on every build. The Λ trust score is Conjecture 1 — advisory, never a gate, never shown as "green".

What if the model isn't sure?

It refuses rather than guesses. Trust is capped and never claimed at 100%; below the confidence bar the system abstains and says why.
